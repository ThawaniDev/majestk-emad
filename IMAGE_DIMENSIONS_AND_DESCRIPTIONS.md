## COLOR SCHEME GUIDELINES

For all images, maintain consistency with these general guidelines:
- **Primary Colors**: Professional blues, grays, or brand-specific colors
- **Accent Colors**: Complementary accent colors for CTAs and highlights
- **Style**: Modern, clean, professional
- **Mood**: Confident, trustworthy, innovative
- **Lighting**: Natural, well-lit, professional photography standards

## IMAGE QUALITY STANDARDS

- **Resolution**: High quality, minimum 72 DPI for web (150 DPI preferred)
- **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for logos/icons with transparency
- **Optimization**: Compress for web while maintaining quality
- **Aspect Ratios**: Maintain exact dimensions listed above
- **Consistency**: All images should have consistent color grading and professional look
